## About

MeowQuiz is a single-player, web-based quiz game with **multiple rounds**:  

1. Easy  
2. Mid  
3. Hard  
4. Player-chosen subject  
5. Penalty round (wrong answers = -2 points)  

- Questions cover **general knowledge**, **science**, **geography**, **technology**, and **fun facts**.  
- Cute **ASCII cat mascots** guide you through the game.  
- High score is saved locally to track your best score.  
- Fully playable in **desktop and mobile browsers**.  

---

## **Features**

- Aesthetic, interactive interface with **animated ASCII cats**.  
- Multiple difficulty levels with balanced questions.  
- Player can choose a subject for a custom round.  
- Penalty round adds extra challenge.  
- High score tracking for replayability.  
- Fully **HTML + JS**, no extra software required.  

---

## **How to Play**

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser.  
2. Play through the rounds, answering questions by clicking or typing **A, B, C, or D**.  
3. After the rounds, see your **final score** and **high score**.  

**Tip:** On mobile, use a browser in portrait mode for best experience.
